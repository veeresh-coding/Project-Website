Welcome to Smart Helmet

A smart, affordable safety solution that detects crashes and alerts emergency contacts automatically — saving lives on the road.

Real World Problem Explanation

Many riders die due to delayed help or not wearing helmets. Current systems don’t detect accidents or alert emergency services automatically, causing critical time loss that could have saved lives.

Proposed Idea

The Smart Helmet is a low-cost solution that detects crashes using motion and impact sensors, then sends an SOS with the rider’s GPS location through Bluetooth or GSM. A 30-second cancel option prevents false alarms. Built with affordable parts (₹1,500–₹4,000), it ensures faster help and improved survival rates.

How It Works

When sensors detect a crash, the helmet vibrates and beeps for 30 seconds. If not canceled, it sends an SOS message and Google Maps link to emergency contacts. It may also flash an emergency light or call automatically for quick response.
